Bonus pool: 5% of monthly gross White Glove revenue, cash collected (not invoiced), net of channel partner and affiliate fees. Paid monthly.

Membership conversion: every White Glove member you convert before their final payment is collected counts toward the pool as a $10,000 placement fee. A conversion is a bigger lift than finishing a placement, and the credit reflects that.
How the Pool Splits

Each person holds a number of shares, and the pool divides proportionally.

Manager / Lead2×
Talent Advisor1×
New hireramping quarterly → 1×
New hires ramp over 12 months before reaching a full 1× share. The team lead sets the exact ramp schedule, the partial share during ramp, and the timing to a full share, for each new hire.
How & When You Get Paid
All commissions are paid in arrears, in the period after the commissionable event is collected.

Trigger: the monthly pool is calculated on cash received.
