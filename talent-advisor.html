Your base salary covers a production floor of $20K in closed fees per month. You earn 5% of every dollar of closed volume above that floor.

Fee size is what counts, not headcount. Closing two $8K technical roles counts the same as several $3K admin roles toward the floor.

Need a specialist (tech, finance) to close a hard role? Loop them in. That’s what base salary funds.
How & When You Get Paid
All commissions are paid in arrears, in the period after the commissionable event is collected.

Trigger: the hire closes and is collected, above your floor.

Guarantee periods. If a deal carries a guarantee window or pay-over-time plan, it counts toward your floor immediately, but payment is held until the member clears the window. During that window you work with the recruiting team to protect the placement.
