Core / Key Accounts

Talent Advisor

You close roles. Your comp is individual, no pool.
Base covers this
5% of every $ above
$0 $20K floor →

Your base salary covers a production floor of $20K in closed fees per month. Above that, you earn 5% of every dollar of closed volume above $20K, paid in the month you earn it.

Fee size is what counts, not headcount. Closing two $8K technical roles counts the same as several $3K admin roles toward the floor.

Need a specialist (tech, finance) to close a hard role? Loop them in. That’s what base salary funds.
Fulfillment

White Glove Team

Collaborative work, so the bonus is pooled.

Bonus pool: 5% of monthly gross White Glove revenue, cash collected (not invoiced), net of channel partner and affiliate fees. Paid monthly.

Candidates move across the team freely, and the pool reflects that.

Membership conversion: every White Glove member you convert before their final payment is collected counts toward the pool as a $10,000 placement fee. A conversion is a bigger lift than finishing a placement, and the credit reflects that.

Guarantee periods

If a deal carries a guarantee window or pay-over-time plan, it counts toward your quota immediately, but payment is held until the member clears the window. During that window you work with the recruiting team to protect the placement. Your payout depends on it.

Membership cancellations

Members have a defined early-exit window. Money collected for work already performed is not returned. Your commission is paid once the member clears the window. A cancellation inside it means the commission is not paid. Offering a guarantee is at the sales rep’s discretion: it can help close a marginal sale, but it delays the payout.
