Account Credit

By default, once an account’s floor is reached, you get credit for every hire that flows through it. Sagan may split larger accounts across multiple strategists at its discretion, particularly for larger companies.

If you think you should get credit for a hire that did not go through your form, let management know and we’ll decide case by case.

How & When You Get Paid
All commissions are paid in arrears, in the period after the commissionable event is collected.

Trigger: the hire closes and Sagan is paid.

Guarantee periods. If a deal carries a guarantee window or pay-over-time plan, it counts toward your floor immediately, but payment is held until the member clears the window. During that window you work with the recruiting team to protect the placement.
