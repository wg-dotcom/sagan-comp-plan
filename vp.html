Accelerators kick in once you pass five deals in a calendar month. They are not retroactive, so only the deals after quota earn the higher rate. The goal is to keep you pushing all month rather than parking deals.

Channel partner fees, affiliate fees, and discounts do not reduce your flat commission.

How & When You Get Paid
All commissions are paid in arrears, in the period after the commissionable event is collected.
EventWhat triggers your commission
White Glove saleDeposit collected; payout begins once the placement fee is paid
Membership saleFirst month’s payment, unless a guarantee is in place

Guarantee periods. If a deal carries a guarantee window or pay-over-time plan, it counts toward your quota immediately, but payment is held until the member clears the window. During that window you work with the recruiting team to protect the placement. Your payout depends on it.

Membership cancellations. Members have a defined early-exit window. Money collected for work already performed is not returned. Your commission is paid once the member clears the window. A cancellation inside it means the commission is not paid. Offering a guarantee is at your discretion: it can help close a marginal sale, but it delays the payout.
